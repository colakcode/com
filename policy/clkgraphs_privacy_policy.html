Explanation

This document presents what kind of user data our "clkGraphs - Chart Maker" application, which we offer to you as CLK Apps, needs and how your data is protected.


Purpose of Collection of Personal Data

clkGraphs may ask you to signup to the app during use. The sign up process is completely left to the user's decision. In our application, you can create an account with an e-mail address and a password, or you can log in with your google account defined on your android phone. If you have created an account other than your Google account, you will receive a confirmation e-mail to your e-mail address. After verifying your e-mail address, your account can be used actively. If you are logged in with your Google account, verification will not be required.


Signing in to our application will give you some advantages. It allows you to back up the data you use to create charts to your account, and to return to your most recent data backup when you accidentally delete an important record or records. It also allows you to synchronize your data on multiple phones at the same time (by logging in with the same account on all phones you have) and access the data backed up to your account on your new phone when you change your phone.


Another advantage of the account creation process is that when you purchase premium content such as "Remove Ads", the content is matched to your e-mail address and you can use premium on all phones with the same account.


Data Requested by Account Creation

- With your Google Account; email, name, language
- With your e-mail address; e-mail, password (that you defined for clkGraphs) data is requested.
- Moreover ; phone make-model, device language, operating system version

Graphical data; They are structures consisting of qualitative data (such as name, color, graphic type) and numerical data. Graphical data and account data collected within the application are stored on the servers of CLK Apps and are never shared with third parties or companies. All collected data has no purpose other than to provide and increase application functionality.
