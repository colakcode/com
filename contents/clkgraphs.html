clkGraphs - Chart Maker

Posted on June 1, 2021

With the clkGraphs app, you can easily create column, line, area, bar, pie, donut, and radar charts. You can sort your graphics in groups and make full-screen presentations. clkGraphs application allows you to create graphs from an android tablet or phone without the need for office programs on your computer. If you log in with your mail account, you can save your data online. You can update your variables and chart elements at any time.
