CLK Budget Manager

Posted on Feb 10, 2019

The CLK Budget Manager application has been developed to record your individual / corporate (small-scale) income and expenditures, to follow them in statistics, to calculate your current bank account balances, to track your loan payments and to easily see your credit card expenditures. If you are having trouble keeping track of your spending during the month, the Budget Management application will help you with its easy and convenient interface. You will also notice as you use it that it has facilities that many other applications in the store do not offer.
What You Can Do With CLK Budget Manager Application. With CLK Budget Manager application, you can easily keep all your income and expense records. You can compare your total income and expenses under monthly, annual and specific dates, view your income and expenses in statistics, and track your credit card expenditures and card payments. You can create reminders for your records and remind you of your important income and payments. By sorting your income and expenses by monthly, weekly or specific dates and categories, you can see the total amounts between the selected dates. You can save the listed results to your phone by pouring them into excel environment. At the same time, you can send your excel file to your e-mail address using sharing options without saving on the phone.
